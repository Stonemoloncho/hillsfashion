Contemporary apparel, footwear, and accessories market event featuring a curated mix of men’s and gender fluid collections, ranging from premium to affordable luxury price points.



Showcasing a fresh point-of-view for the contemporary market in an intimate and new setting, PROJECT New York features a curated presentation of men’s and gender fluid apparel, footwear, and accessories brands. Drawing an exclusive audience of key retailers, thought leaders, and influential voices in North America’s fashion capital, PROJECT New York returns to its downtown roots and brings to life the intersection of community, culture, and fashion.
